With the growth of ICPC in Asia Pacific countries and around the world, from the cycle 2023-2024, the ICPC Asia Pacific will change from a three-tier system (Preliminary, Regional, World Finals) to a four-tier system (Preliminary, Regional, Super-Regional, World Finals). The super-regional tier of ICPC Asia Pacific will be called "The ICPC Asia Pacific Championship."

As this Super-Regional is invitational only, students from universities in West Pacific and Southeast Asia, including South Korea, Japan, Taiwan, Vietnam, Thailand, Malaysia, Philippines, Indonesia, Cambodia, Myanmar, Singapore, and Laos, will be able to participate in the ICPC Asia Pacific Championship through the regional contests. For more information on regional contests, visit the ICPC Regional Finder.

The Premier Global Coding Competition

Since 1977, the International Collegiate Programming Contest (ICPC) has stood as the world's most prestigious competitive programming event for university students, challenging generations of brilliant minds. It's more than just a contest; it's a proving ground for the next generation of tech leaders, software engineers, and innovative problem-solvers who will shape the future of our industry.

Powered by a Mission-Driven Foundation

The ICPC Foundation is a 501(c)(3) non-profit dedicated to advancing the art and science of competitive programming to benefit society. Under their guidance, the ICPC fosters a global community of innovators through sponsorship, outreach, and this legendary contest. The Foundation's work ensures the competition remains accessible, impactful, and at the absolute forefront of discovering and nurturing elite computational talent from every corner of the globe.
