# Part of the International Collegiate Programming Contest (ICPC)

<div id="icpc-foundation" class="flex-container">
    <div class="text-container" style="padding-right: 20px;">
        <h3>The Premier Global Coding Competition</h3>
        <p>Since 1977, the <a href="https://icpc.global/">International Collegiate Programming Contest (ICPC)</a> has stood as the world's most prestigious competitive programming event for university students, challenging generations of brilliant minds. It's more than just a contest; it's a proving ground for the next generation of tech leaders, software engineers, and innovative problem-solvers who will shape the future of our industry.</p>
        <h3>Powered by a Mission-Driven Foundation</h3>
        <p>The <a href="https://icpc.foundation/">ICPC Foundation</a> is a 501(c)(3) non-profit dedicated to advancing the art and science of competitive programming to benefit society. Under their guidance, the ICPC fosters a global community of innovators through sponsorship, outreach, and this legendary contest. The Foundation's work ensures the competition remains accessible, impactful, and at the absolute forefront of discovering and nurturing elite computational talent from every corner of the globe.</p>
