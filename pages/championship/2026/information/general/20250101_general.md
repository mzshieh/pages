Top 65 teams (195 distinct contestants and ≈40+ coaches from ≈40+ distinct Universities) from 5 other ICPC Asia Regionals in Nov-Dec 2024 (see below), inclusive of 2 invited teams from South Pacific Regionals have been invited for the 2025 ICPC Asia Pacific Championship as per [ICPC Asia Pacific Championship (2024-25 cycle) selection rules](https://icpc.iisf.or.jp/asia-pacific/2024-2025-cycle/).

1. [**Sydney**/**Australia**](https://sppcontests.org/icpc-south-pacific-regional-finals-2024/) (Sun, 20 Oct 2024, [scoreboard](https://sppcontests.org/scoreboards/2024/regionals/scoreboard.htm)),
2. [**Taichung**/**Taiwan**](https://hpc.asia.edu.tw/icpc2024/) (Sun, 17 Nov 2024, [scoreboard](https://taichung2024.icpc.tw/)),
3. [**Seoul**/**South Korea**](https://icpckorea.org/) (Sat, 23 Nov 2024, [scoreboard](http://static.icpckorea.net/2024/regional/scoreboard/)),
4. [**Jakarta**/**Indonesia**](https://competition.binus.ac.id/icpc2024/) (Sun, 01 Dec 2024, [scoreboard](https://competition.binus.ac.id/icpc2024/final.html)),
5. [**Hanoi**/**Vietnam**](https://www.olp.vn/) (Fri, 13 Dec 2024, [scoreboard](https://icpc.vnoi.info/contest/icpc/ranking/)),
6. [**Yokohama**/**Japan**](https://icpc.iisf.or.jp/2024-yokohama/) (Sun, 22 Dec 2024, [scoreboard](https://icpcsec.firebaseapp.com/standings)).

See the participating teams of this Super-Regional in the [teams](/competition/teams) page.
