## Rules

1. Wear the provided contest T-shirt as the outermost garment and the badge at all times, including during contests. 
  * Contest T-shirts and badges will be distributed during the onsite registration.
  * Jackets, undershirts, or any other clothing are allowed to be worn inside the provided contest T-shirt.
2. All contestants are encouraged to wear shoes.
3. Do not touch anything on the work station before the contest starts.
4. Do not eat inside the Contest Hall. You can only drink plain water (use the provided drinking bottle provided by Huawei/Jump Trading) inside the Contest Hall.
  * Snacks and other packet drinks are provided outside the Contest Hall. Please consume them outside.
  * Do not bring any snacks or drinks of your own. Do not submit them on the dress rehearsal.
5. Do not bring any electronic/communication devices.
  * Laptops, tablets, keyboards, mice, cameras, calculators, digital dictionaries, USB memory sticks/cards, watches, radios, mobile phones, smartphones, earphones, headphones, pagers, and other electronic/communication devices are prohibited.
  * Electronic medical aid that has to be worn by a contestant during the contest is permitted, but such an item needs to be (privately) declared during the registration.
6. Please be considerate of other teams and compete fairly.
  * A team may be **disqualified** if the team is found to be cheating. Cheating involves any attempt or activity by an individual or groups (team members and/or coach) to enhance or diminish their performance or other teams' performance by means other than actual ability related to the contest, including but not limited to efforts such as submitting a solution which is not their own, hacking (successful or not) the contest management system or other machines, dislodging extension cords, or other distracting behaviours. All teams should uphold the sportsmanship and the spirit of fair competition.
