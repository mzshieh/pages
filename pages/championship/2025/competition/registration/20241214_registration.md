Qualified teams (the top side of each regionals above) will be automatically promoted to the contest in the ICPC global system. The coach of qualified teams will be contacted by the organiser to confirm the intention to participate and follow up with the registration process. This process is expected to be done by end Dec 2024 after ICPC Yokohama regionals and to be completed latest by mid Jan 2025.

Accepted teams then need to pay a registration fee of 280 SGD (it is 200 USD - same as ICPC APAC Championship 2024 - but with additional of 9% Singapore Goods and Servies Tax (GST), rounded to nearest tenth after conversion to SGD) per team via [method TBC by end Dec 2024].
