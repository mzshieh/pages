## Team Invitation

Qualified teams have been promoted to the 2025 ICPC Asia Pacific Championship in the [ICPC global system](https://icpc.global/regionals/finder/APSEPC).

The coach of qualified teams have been contacted by the organiser to confirm the intention to participate and to follow up with the registration process.

## Registration Fee

Accepted teams need to pay a registration fee of **280 SGD** per team.

Detailed instructions on how to pay (electronically) using SoCPay system have been emailed by Ms Irene Ong on Wed, 08 Jan 2025.

## Invitation Letters

Any team coach with member(s) that need Singapore visa and/or formal invitation letter (for budget/official leave application) should contact the RCD as soon as possible.

Official invitation letters will be issued to teams who have formally accepted the invitation from Thu, 02 Jan 2025 onwards.
